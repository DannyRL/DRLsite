    • A little bit about myself...



    My name is Daniel. I'm a senior studying Computer Engineering with a hardware emphasis at the University of Florida. I was born in Virginia Beach, Virginia where I lived for 14 years until I moved to Jacksonville, Florida. I lived in Jacksonville all through high school and then moved to Gainesville, Florida to study at UF. Fun fact: I went to the same highschool Tim Tebow went to.

    I'm Filipino. My last name is pronounced "Leh-liss". I'm a bit of a shy and introverted person, but I'm always willing to meet and work with new people.

    I hope to use the skills I've picked up here at UF to start a career working on the next generation of electronics and machines. Most of my technical experience is with microprocessor applications and embedded systems, so I hope to get a job doing something along those lines.
